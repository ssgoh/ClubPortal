Step 1: Your Digital Passport

To use ThingSpeak, you first have to join the service by creating a free account.

  • Visit ThingSpeak.com and click "Sign Up".
  • Verification: You must click the link in your email to activate the account.
2

Step 2: Create a Data "Vault"

Create a Channel in the service to receive our data from the sensor.

New Channel Settings
Name: Library Monitor
Field 1: Temperature
Field 2: Humidity
  1. Click Channels > My Channels > New Channel.
  2. Give it a Name and enable Field 1 and Field 2.
  3. Click Save Channel at the bottom.
