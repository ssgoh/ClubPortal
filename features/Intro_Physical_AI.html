What is Physical AI?

It's the bridge between software and reality. Physical AI connects sensors to intelligent decisions and real-world actions.

Sensor

Reads temp, motion, or light

AI Brain

Processes and decides

Action

Triggers fans or alerts

Absolutely not. This session is designed to explain the concepts. We will handle the code behind the scenes so you can focus on how it all works together.

Just your curiosity! We provide all the sensors, devices, and materials for the live demonstrations.

This session is specifically crafted to be age-inclusive. Whether you are 15 or 85, if you want to know how the modern world works, you belong here.
